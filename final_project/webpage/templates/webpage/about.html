Our CPEN 291 Final Project

People enjoy listening to music during different times of the day and accordingly, choose specific genres of music based on their current mood and feelings. This project focusses on creating an application that is able to detect the mood (e.g. sadness, anger, happiness, contempt, etc.) based on facial expression, such as mouth, eyes, and eyebrows. The application requires the user to input an image of their face through the application, where we will use computer vision techniques to determine the mood the individual is in and accordingly output a list of songs or playlists for the user to listen to on Spotify. For instance, if the model determines the user is angry, the output will show this result along with a randomly generated playlist that contains peaceful and soothing music.
